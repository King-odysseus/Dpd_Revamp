We are a freelancing platform that connects African Technical Experts to remote work in the development space.

From technical writing to proposal development, to survey implementation, digital health research, Dpdonline has everything you need to get the job done faster, better and with less cost.

We have a comprehensive pool of technical experts categorized by thematic areas, skillset and experience level. No more endless searches for consultants for your projects, no more long hiring cycles.

Our freelance consultants get to benefit from greater visibility to clients, an additional income source and greater agency over their skills and time. Short term gigs for single consultants, managed services and full hiring available.

All technical areas in the development sector under one roof

Single Gig
This option is ideal if you have a larger project that requires a managed team of experts to complete. DPD helps you find a team of consultants who have the skills and expertise needed to successfully complete your project. By outsourcing your project to a team, you can ensure that all aspects of the project are covered and completed to a high standard. Once an agreement has been reached with DPD, a set of milestones are agreed upon. 100% of the milestone payment is made into an Escrow account with Payoneer before the work commences. Client is able to request a number of revisions to the deliverable and may request a refund for unsatisfactory work.
Managed Services
This subscription only option is perfect if you need to hire a consultant to work with your organization on a long-term basis. You can easily find and hire a consultant who has the skills and experience needed to help your organization grow and succeed. By hiring a consultant for a role in your organization, you can benefit from their expertise and knowledge on a longer term.
Fulltime Hires
This option is perfect if you have a specific task that requires the expertise of a single consultant. You can easily find a consultant who has the specific skills you need to get the job done and engage. Once an agreement has been reached, full payment is made into an Escrow account with Payoneer. Client is able to request a number of revisions to the deliverable and may request a refund for unsatisfactory work. Upon successful completion and confirmation by client, the consultant receives 80% of the fees while DPD collects a 20% commission for the service.
